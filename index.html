About the Conference

FISAT Business School proudly hosts the FISAT Business School International Conference on Management Research (FICMR) 2026.

The conference brings together academics, researchers, policymakers and industry experts to discuss sustainable management practices and innovative research across multiple disciplines.

Objective

To promote research collaboration and knowledge sharing on sustainable management practices among global scholars and industry professionals.

Vision

To reshape management theories and practices by integrating sustainability, innovation and responsible leadership.
